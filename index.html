Who She Is

The Journalist

Lennon Spektor is a broadcast journalist, executive producer, and storyteller based in Miami Beach, Florida. By the time most students are applying to college, Lennon had already won three Regional Student Emmy Awards — a distinction most professional journalists never achieve.

At Gulliver Preparatory School, she serves as Executive Producer of Raider Rise and Chief of Feature News at RaiderVision, the school's award-winning broadcast program. She doesn't just appear on camera — she runs the entire operation. Editorial planning, production timelines, scripting, interviews, B-roll, final edits. Every week.

Outside the studio, she teaches journalism to middle schoolers through Breakthrough Miami, translating professional newsroom standards into lessons that stick. She brings the same discipline to an e-commerce business she founded and continues to operate.

About Lennon

The Full Picture

Lennon Spektor is a junior at Gulliver Preparatory School in Miami Beach, Florida, where she is enrolled in the school's Digital Mass Media and Business and Entrepreneurship signature programs. She has built one of the most decorated high school broadcast journalism portfolios in the country.

Her three Regional Student Emmy wins are not just impressive for a teenager — they are impressive for anyone. She earned them through the same work ethic that drives everything she does: showing up early, staying late, holding her team to a high standard, and refusing to submit anything that isn't ready.

She is a member of the National Honor Society, National English Honor Society, and Quill and Scroll International Journalism Honor Society. She is a certified Red Cross Lifeguard. She runs a business. She teaches kids. She makes great television.

She is exactly the kind of student who arrives on a college campus and immediately makes it better.
